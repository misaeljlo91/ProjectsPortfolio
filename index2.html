About me

I consider myself a very curious person, therefore, I am an autodidact by nature, I like to read and investigate. Learning new things has been my personal projection.

I studied engineering in Venezuela, after looking for work in my profession I was presented with the opportunity to work in the marketing coordination of an institute, acquiring new skills in a field completely new for me at that time. After that experience my interest in the world of technology began to grow, this allowed me to do a Master in Marketing Digital and Web analytics, using WordPress and its kits, and creating my own blog.

One day the opportunity presented itself to me in MindHub to realise a bootcamp and this helped me to make the decision to do Front-End development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Vue.js, Bootstrap), acquiring skills and capacities too in the Back-End development (JAVA, Spring Boot, Spring Security, Gradle, PostgreSQL).

I would like to specialize in the design UX/UI but without close myself to the opportunity of learn new skills and continue training as a Full Stack Web Developer. I know that a day I will be able to exceed all expectations I have for myself and my future.

Ball D'or

This project included the creation of an online store that in turn was connected to the banking platform that was previously developed. It was a group project where five (05) people participated, each one with a role within the project and, of free decision regarding its content but complying with the guidelines established for its development.

It was developed in a short time and applying the knowledge of Back-End and Front-End that we acquired during our training in the bootcamp.

In this project, the Git tool and the GitHub platform were used to push each of the changes and modifications that were made throughout its development, Java was used as the language for the Back-End development and where they were made models and controllers to be used in the Front-End; regarding the development of the user view, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap and Vue.js were used.

My role within the team was the visual development or the Front-End part, in the same way to add certain functionalities that were implemented I had to develop API Rest services of that functionalities, including the option of Forgot password, change user's data and change password.
